# Chapter 09 – Evolution of the Diagnostic Tool

## 9.1 From Deterministic v1 to Stochastic v2

The original compass was deterministic — fixed scores, fixed shocks. v2 introduces small noise (±5–10%) on metrics to simulate real-world drift. Test on ants (low variance) vs. VOC late stage (high variance).

## 9.2 Bifurcation Analyzer

Track when small metric deltas push a replicator across attractor boundaries (e.g., mutualism ellipsoid → parasitism sinkhole). Use ensemble runs to map tipping points.

## 9.3 Longevity Fits & Ensemble Statistics

Fit Weibull/Gompertz distributions to ensemble durations (100 runs per replicator with noise). Report median longevity, confidence intervals, and hazard rates.

Ends with: “The microscope now sees noise, tipping points, and decay curves — ready for navigation.”
