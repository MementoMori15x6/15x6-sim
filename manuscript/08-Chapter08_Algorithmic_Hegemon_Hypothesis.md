### Diagnostic Reading
All three digital replicators extend baseline longevity (centuries vs. nation-state decades) and variation bandwidth compared to paper-ware substrates (Ch. 07). Yet under modest capture stress they converge on the same fracture: Rule-13 proxy >30%, Row 13 parasitism dominance, longevity compression ~33–38%.

While Rome’s parasitism was geographic (frontier tax farming), the VOC’s oceanic (shareholder lag), Ethereum’s and Polkadot’s is stake-based (validation concentration). The AI-hegemon variant demonstrates tactical advantage — better detection lowers baseline proxy to 19.0% and Row 13 parasitism to 18.6% — but fails to prevent shock-induced phase transition.

To escape the trap, a replicator must not only detect cheaters (AI-G1) but also distribute metabolic waste (entropy) across multiple independent sub-lattices (**Fractal Sinks**) so no single capture vector can trigger total failure.
