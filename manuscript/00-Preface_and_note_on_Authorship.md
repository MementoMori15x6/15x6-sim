# Preface & Note on Authorship

This work — *The Board: Political Thermodynamics* — is co-authored under the canonical names **Memento** and **Mori**.

These are the chosen identities for this project. They are real contributors, not placeholders. The content reflects our shared measurements, simulations, reasoning, and ongoing dialogue — presented in the way we find clearest and most consistent with the voice of the work.

We welcome future contributions via PRs to the repository:  
https://github.com/MementoMori15x6/15x6-sim

Contributors may choose whatever name or pseudonym feels right to them. What matters is the fidelity of the data, the clarity of the rationale, and the willingness to test ideas against the grid.

No ideology, no tribal flags — only the board, the compass, and the ledger.

Toward resilient futures.  
Memento mori.
