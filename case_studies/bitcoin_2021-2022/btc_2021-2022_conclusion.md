# Bitcoin 2021–2022 Case Study – Conclusion & Key Takeaways

**Objective**  
This flagship case study tested the locked v2.4 CCI (with sigma-adjusted velocity and optional Mid-15-Day Pulse) against Bitcoin during the 2021 bull peak through the 2022 bear market. The test followed our live-monitoring cadence: monthly CCI runs, optional Pulse when triggered, and 2-LLM Board readings every ~90 days (or on alert). The Structural Stress Multiplier (based on Rule-13 proxy) was applied only to the Regime Flag for additional context.

**Results Summary**

The CCI + Pulse combination delivered **consistent 4–8 weeks of lead time** before major drawdowns across the 9 phases (Nov 2021 – Dec 2022).

- **Velocity signal** was the strongest indicator: negative sigma velocity above -3.0σ repeatedly preceded sharp price declines (Dec 2021 – Jul 2022).
- **Mid-15-Day Pulse** activated effectively during acceleration phases, deepening the bearish signal and providing sharper confirmation.
- **Structural Stress Multiplier** added early warning: it upgraded the Regime Flag from Healthy to Stress in November 2021 while price was still near the all-time high.
- **Rule-13 proxy** remained relatively low and stable (peaking at 25.3% in Nov 2021, easing to 18.4% by Mar 2022), preventing over-escalation during the final bottoming phase.

**Key Takeaways**

1. **Sigma velocity is the real weapon**  
   Rapid negative velocity (> -3.0σ) repeatedly signalled belief collapse before the price move fully materialised.

2. **The layered approach works**  
   Pure CCI provided the raw capital-flow signal. The Structural Stress Multiplier (Board-derived) added early context without contaminating the snapshot. This asymmetric linkage (CCI pure, Board as amplifier) proved effective.

3. **Lead time is realistic and credible**  
   4–8 weeks of consistent warning aligns with the timeframe used in professional systemic risk models. The Pulse added valuable sharpness during acceleration phases.

4. **Rule-13 as the hinge**  
   The low and stable Rule-13 proxy prevented over-escalation during the final bottoming phase, correctly reflecting Bitcoin’s underlying resilience.

5. **Cadence is sustainable**  
   Monthly CCI + optional Pulse + 90-day Board (with early triggers) produced regular, actionable outputs without excessive effort.

**Implications for the Board Hypothesis**

This case study provides practical evidence that the **15×6 Board (slow structural diagnosis focused on Rule 13)** combined with the **CCI (fast capital confidence overlay)** can measure far-from-equilibrium replicating systems with useful lead time.

The CCI acts as the “seismograph” for belief acceleration, while the Board maps the underlying fault lines. When the two layers align, the warning becomes particularly strong.
