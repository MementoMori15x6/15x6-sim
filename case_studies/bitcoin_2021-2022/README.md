# Bitcoin 2021–2022 Case Study

**Objective**  
Demonstrate consistent lead time from the CCI + Mid-15-Day Pulse overlay on the most watched crypto asset (Bitcoin) during the 2021 bull peak through the 2022 bear market.

This case study serves as the flagship demonstration of the Board + CCI framework before moving to 2025–2026 live monitoring.

**Scope**
- Monthly CCI runs using locked v2.4 (sigma velocity as primary metric + Regime Flag)
- Optional Mid-15-Day Pulse when triggered or recommended by the main CCI
- Light Board linkage (2-LLM consensus or single-run) every ~90 days or on CCI alert
- Clear timelines showing CCI warning dates vs actual BTC price/MV action
- Explicit lead time in weeks before major moves

**Methodology**
- CCI: Pure standalone 30-day snapshots (v2.4)
- Board: 2-LLM consensus (escalated to 4-LLM on Danger/Run Risk)
- All outputs clearly mark historical data
- Goal: Show verifiable multi-week lead time to illustrate the Board hypothesis in practice

**Live Monitoring Cadence (Locked)**
- Board reading: every 90 days (2-LLM consensus) or triggered early on CCI Danger/Run Risk
- CCI: every 30 days (standalone)
- Mid-15-Day Pulse: on request or strong recommendation from main CCI
- MC runs: reserved for major stage transitions or high-alert periods only

**Files in this folder**
- CCI monthly outputs
- Pulse outputs (when triggered)
- Board readings
- Combined timeline with BTC price action
- Lead-time summary
