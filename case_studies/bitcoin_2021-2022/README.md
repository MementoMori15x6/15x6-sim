# Bitcoin 2021–2022 Case Study

**Objective**  
Demonstrate consistent lead time from the CCI + Mid-15-Day Pulse overlay on the most watched crypto asset (Bitcoin) during the 2021 bull peak through the 2022 bear market.

**Scope**
- Monthly CCI runs using locked v2.4 (sigma velocity + Regime Flag)
- Optional Mid-15-Day Pulse when triggered
- Light Board linkage (2-LLM consensus or single-run) every ~90 days or on CCI alert
- Clear timelines showing CCI warning dates vs actual MV/price action
- Explicit lead time in weeks

**Methodology**
- CCI: Pure standalone 30-day snapshots
- Board: 2-LLM consensus (escalated to 4-LLM on high-alert)
- All outputs clearly mark historical data
- Goal: Show verifiable multi-week lead time before major moves to illustrate the Board hypothesis in practice

**Files**
- CCI monthly outputs
- Pulse outputs (when triggered)
- Board readings
- Combined timeline with MV

This case study serves as the flagship demonstration before moving to 2025–2026 live monitoring.
