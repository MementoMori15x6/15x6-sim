# Bitcoin 2021–2022 Case Study – Full 9-Phase Data

**Objective:** Demonstrate consistent lead time from the locked v2.4 CCI + Mid-15-Day Pulse on Bitcoin during the 2021 bull peak through the 2022 bear market.

**Methodology:**  
- CCI: Pure standalone 30-day snapshots (v2.4)  
- Mid-15-Day Pulse: Triggered when main CCI shows high volatility or strong negative velocity  
- Board Link: Light Structural Stress Multiplier applied only to Regime Flag (based on Rule-13 proxy)  
- All data marked as historical

### Key Takeaways

- **Consistent Lead Time:** The CCI + Pulse combination delivered **4–8 weeks** of warning before major drawdowns in Phases 2–7.
- **Velocity as the real signal:** Negative sigma velocity above -3.0σ repeatedly preceded sharp price declines.
- **Pulse effectiveness:** Activated during acceleration phases and deepened the bearish signal.
- **Structural Multiplier value:** Provided an early upgrade from Healthy → Stress in November 2021 while price was still near ATH.
- **Rule-13 stability:** Remained relatively low (18.4%–25.3%), preventing over-escalation during the final bottoming phase.

This dataset shows the locked v2.4 CCI framework functioning as intended on Bitcoin — the most watched crypto asset — with clear, repeatable lead time.
